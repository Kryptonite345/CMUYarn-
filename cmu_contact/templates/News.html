CMU Cheers for Mayor Jeannie Sandoval, Asian Hero!

The City of Malabon University (CMU) community overflows with pride celebrating our Chairman of the Board of Regents, Mayor Jeannie Sandoval's momentous achievement. Featured in "Asian Heroes 2024," a prestigious coffee table book by Dr. Maria Rosa "Bing" Carion, this honor is a testament to Mayor Sandoval's exceptional leadership and unwavering dedication to Malabon City.

CMU beams with pride as Mayor Sandoval joins the ranks of Asia's outstanding leaders and changemakers. "Asian Heroes 2024" spotlights individuals making significant contributions, championing positive change, and advocating for women and youth.

As Chairman of the CMU Board of Regents, Mayor Sandoval's visionary leadership shapes the university's path towards continued growth. Her guidance is invaluable in providing quality education and creating opportunities for CMU.

Congratulations from the entire CMU family to Mayor Sandoval on this remarkable achievement!

CMU Community Showed Resilience in Nationwide Earthquake Drill

The CMU community, from students and staffs to professors and administrators, participated enthusiastically in the 4th Quarter Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill.

The drill held on Thursday, November 14, 2024, at 2:00 PM, the entire CMU community practiced the "Duck, Cover and Hold" protocol, simulating a response to a potential earthquake.

The drill served as a valuable opportunity to reinforce earthquake safety procedures, promote awareness, and foster a culture of resilience within the CMU community. As one community came together for this crucial drill, it demonstrated a collective commitment to safety and well-being in the face of potential disasters.
